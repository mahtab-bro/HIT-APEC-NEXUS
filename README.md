# HIT APEC-NEXUS ⬡

NEXUS is a gamified productivity and habit-building platform designed to transform daily routines into rewarding quests. Originally conceptualized for students at the **Harbin Institute of Technology, Shenzhen (HITSZ)**, it is built to foster a culture of excellence through structural accountability, peer competition, and visible personal growth. 

While tailored for the academic environment, NEXUS is adaptable for users of any age who wish to compete, track their progress, and commit to meaningful self-improvement.

## 🚀 The Core Philosophy
NEXUS addresses the common struggle of procrastination and lack of engagement in extracurricular growth. By combining real-world verification with gaming mechanics, we make "doing the work" as addictive as playing a game.

## 🌟 Key Features

### 🎮 Gamification Mechanics
* **Proof of Work Verification:** Users must upload photographic or screenshot evidence to validate task completion before rewards are issued. No shortcuts.
* **Progression Tiers:** Earned Experience Points (XP) dynamically advance users through ranks, ranging from "Newcomer" to "Legend."
* **Retention Loops:** Stay consistent with a visual daily "flame" streak and prevent system abuse with a 20-hour quest cooldown timer.
* **Economy & Competition:** Earn Gold and XP that feed directly into a live, device-shared campus Leaderboard to fuel friendly rivalry.

### 📚 Quest Categories
NEXUS encourages holistic development across five pillars:
* **Academic & Skills:** Deep work sessions, portal grade tracking, coding practice, and online certifications.
* **Health & Wellness:** Gym/fitness sessions, nutritious eating, meditation, and personal journaling.
* **Community Culture:** Campus club activities, volunteering, and peer-to-peer tutoring.

## 🛠 Tech Stack
* **Frontend:** HTML5, CSS3 (Modern Flex/Grid Layouts), JavaScript (ES6+).
* **Storage:** LocalStorage (Privacy-focused, client-side data management).
* **Integration:** ImgBB API for secure proof image hosting, Wikimedia Commons API for dynamic campus-themed backgrounds.
* **Security:** Client-side SHA-256 password hashing.

## 💡 Why NEXUS?
1. **Structural Accountability:** Transform vague goals into concrete, verified daily tasks.
2. **Visible Recognition:** Celebrate daily wins with a progression system that rewards effort.
3. **Campus-Centric:** Specifically designed for the HITSZ ecosystem, encouraging a productive, competitive campus atmosphere.
